Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Peptide Log is built with a simple principle: your health data belongs to you. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it goes.

Data stays on your iPhone by default

All injection logs, weight entries, side effects, and settings are stored locally on your device. Peptide Log does not require an account. We do not operate servers that store your health data.

Optional iCloud sync

iCloud Sync is off by default. When enabled, your data syncs to your own private iCloud account, never to our servers. Sync is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure under your Apple ID. We cannot access your iCloud data.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect, store, or sell your injection or health data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers in the app.
  • We do not require an email address or account to use the app.

Beta waitlist

If you sign up for the beta on this website, we collect only the email address you provide to send you beta access information. You can unsubscribe at any time. Waitlist emails are not linked to your in-app data.

Export and deletion

You can export your data as PDF or CSV at any time from within the app. You can delete all local data from Settings. If iCloud sync is enabled, you may also manage synced data through your Apple ID and iCloud settings.

Children's privacy

Peptide Log is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@peptidelog.app.
