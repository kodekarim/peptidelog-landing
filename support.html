Frequently asked questions

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. By default, everything stays on your iPhone. If you turn on iCloud Sync in Settings, your data syncs to your own private iCloud account — never to our servers.

Do I need an account?

No account required. Open the app and start logging.

How do I export data for my doctor?

Go to Settings → Export for doctor. Choose PDF or CSV and share via the iOS share sheet.

How do I delete all my data?

Settings → Delete All Data. This removes everything stored locally on your device. If iCloud Sync is on, you may also want to check your iCloud storage settings.

Is Peptide Log medical advice?

No. Peptide Log is a logging tool only. It does not provide medical advice, dosing recommendations, or diagnoses. Always follow your healthcare provider's guidance.

How do I join the beta?

Sign up on the homepage with your email. Beta testers get early access and lifetime free access to future premium features.
